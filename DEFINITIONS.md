# Definitions

## Endogenous Variance
Variance generated from within a system’s internal distribution.

## Exogenous Input
Information whose distribution cannot be derived from the system’s internal state.

## Support
The effective region of an action or hypothesis space with non-negligible probability mass.

## Support Contraction
Reduction of effective support over time under optimization pressure.

## Optimization Pressure
Magnitude of selection or reinforcement applied to high-reward regions.
